## 5. Forking guidance

When you fork `bizcity-twin-ai` and intend to **distribute** the fork publicly:

1. **Rename** the fork. Suggested patterns:
   - `myco-twin-ai` (descriptive)
   - `OpenTwin Framework` (generic)
   - Avoid prefix/suffix that includes "BizCity"
2. **Replace** the BizCity logo with your own.
3. **Keep** the upstream attribution in `LICENSE` and source headers (GPL requirement).
4. **Add** a notice: "This is a fork of BizCity Twin AI by Johnny Chu, modified by [you]. Not affiliated with or endorsed by BizCity."
5. **Update** plugin slug, namespace, REST namespace to avoid runtime conflict.
